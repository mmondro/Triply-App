What's Above the Fold?

Header

The header should fully describe the product and be easily understood by only reading the header text. It should convey the main features and benefits, attracting potential customers.

Subheader

Additional details about the product can be provided to persuade the visitor to take action, such as making a purchase. It is important to strike a balance between providing enough information to be helpful, but not overwhelming the visitor with unnecessary details.

Call to Action

The desired action for the visitor (e.g. sign up, purchase) should be concise and include an incentive to encourage them to take it. The button and incentive should be clear and compelling.

Social Proof

Including reputable and relevant logos or mentions of press coverage or well-known customers can establish credibility and trust, increasing the chances of a purchase
